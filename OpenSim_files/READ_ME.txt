About these file:
==================

These are the OpenSim addon module binaries which provide Profiles and Search services for your regions/grid  The have been compiled with the stable OpenSim 0.7.3 release code.  

Simply upload or move these to your precompiled OpenSim's /bin directory prior to launching and make the appropriate changes to your OpenSim.ini and StandaloneCommon.ini -OR- GridCommon.ini files (as noted in the Quick Setup guide) and restart you OpenSim instance(s).
